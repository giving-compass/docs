## Overview

The Nonprofit Impact product provides access to AI-extracted impact data from nonprofit annual reports. Get both compelling human stories and quantitative metrics that demonstrate real-world outcomes.

## What You Get

### Impact Stories

Human transformation narratives extracted from annual reports, including:

- **Emotional Hooks**: Compelling story openings that capture attention
- **Direct Quotes**: Authentic voices from beneficiaries and stakeholders
- **Transformation Arcs**: Before/after states showing real change
- **Power Scores**: AI-generated quality ratings (0-10) to surface the most compelling narratives
- **Source Attribution**: Links back to original annual reports

### Impact Metrics

Quantitative measures that demonstrate organizational effectiveness:

- **Outcome Metrics**: People served, meals distributed, lives changed
- **Geographic Impact**: Where the impact occurred
- **Time Frames**: Annual, quarterly, or program-specific measurements
- **Metric Types**: Automatically categorized for easy filtering
- **Source Context**: Original text snippets from reports

## Key Features

- **Semantic Search**: Find relevant stories using natural language queries like "education outcomes" or "community health"
- **Multi-Organization Browsing**: Discover impact data across thousands of nonprofits
- **Quality Filtering**: Filter stories by power score to find the most compelling narratives
- **EIN-Based Lookup**: Retrieve all impact data for specific organizations
- **Direct Report Links**: Access original source documents for verification

## Use Cases

### Donor Platforms

Help donors discover nonprofits with proven impact through authentic stories and verified metrics.

### Grant Management

Evaluate nonprofit effectiveness using standardized impact data extracted from annual reports.

### Research & Analytics

Aggregate impact metrics across cause areas to identify trends and benchmark performance.

### Marketing & Communications

Source compelling impact stories with proper attribution for campaigns and content.

### Impact Measurement

Track and compare outcomes across similar organizations using consistent data extraction.
